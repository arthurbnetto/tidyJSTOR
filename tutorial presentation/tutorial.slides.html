This packages is a wrapper around tidyverse utilities and text-analisys tools intended for non-frequent R users to be able to realize bibliometric exploratory research of JSTOR datasets. Advanced R users may prefer to use the package solely for data transformation using JSTOR_df() and produce their own visualizations and analysis."

JSTOR DFR: Search Tips and Tricks¶

Boolean Operators

  • AND, OR, NOT, Parentheses
  • ex: model AND (structural OR structuralist)

Wild cards, truncation and proximity

  • bird*; wom?n; debt forgiveness~10; migration^7 geese

Specific phrases

  • "history of economic thought"

Citations in JSTOR? Yes! Let's use the fact that JSTOR realizes full text searches.

  • "Friedman (1953)"; or "The Methodology of Positive Economics"; or "Essays in Positive Economics"
  • "Friedman (1953)" OR "The Methodology of Positive Economics" OR "Essays in Positive Economics"
  • "Friedman" AND ("The Methodology of Positive Economics" OR "Essays in Positive Economics") AND 1953
